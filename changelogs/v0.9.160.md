# v0.9.160 - Sprint 16: Chain Builder Contract Simplification

**Date:** 2026-03-21

## Summary

This release removes the last conceptual duplicate warning from the module-system chain builder by renaming the internal `build()` workflow to `compose()` and updating the single internal caller. The analyzer now keeps its public contract focused on module analysis instead of sharing the same `build` semantic fingerprint as a deep-chain detector.

## Key Changes

1. **Internal Contract Rename**: `module-analyzer/chains/chain-builder.js` now exposes `compose()` for chain generation.
2. **Single Caller Update**: `ModuleAnalyzer` now calls `compose()` instead of `build()`.
3. **Conceptual Duplicate Reduction**: the watcher no longer reports the `build(build:logic:core:chain)` duplicate on the module-system chain builder.

## Validation

- `chain-builder.js` and `ModuleAnalyzer.js` pass `node --check`.
- The only remaining watcher warning in this area is the unrelated `performAction` contract-level duplicate in `validate-imports.js`.
- The module-system chain analysis still produces the same shape of output; only the internal method name changed.
