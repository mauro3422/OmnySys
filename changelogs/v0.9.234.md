# v0.9.234

## Summary
- Added a softer daemon restart ladder with `refresh`, `reload`, `reindex`, and `restart` actions.
- Broke down `BatchProcessor`, `ErrorGuardian`, and the WebSocket client/server path into smaller helpers.
- Removed `statsPool`-based stub stats from WebSocket runtime objects and returned real local snapshots.
- Cleaned the hot-reload restart coordinator and improved runtime-facing restart handling.

## Notes
- The daemon now prefers proxy-managed worker restarts for runtime-facing changes.
- `stale` and `restartRequired` are treated as operational signals, not hard failures, when the supervisor can recover automatically.
