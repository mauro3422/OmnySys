# v0.9.151 - Sprint 15: Call Relation Orchestration Split

**Date:** 2026-03-19

## Summary

This refactor moves call-relation persistence and shared-state linkage out of `link.js` into dedicated helpers so the linker stays focused on orchestration.

## Key Changes

1.  **Call Relation Split**: `saveAtomRelations` now delegates to a dedicated call-relation persistence helper.
2.  **Shared-State Split**: Shared-state linkage moved into its own helper module and the public linker wrappers now remain thin.
3.  **Runtime Safety**: Shared-state paths keep explicit async error boundaries and continue syncing semantic connections from canonical relations.
4.  **Linker Slimming**: `link.js` is materially smaller and easier to reason about, reducing the risk of future AI edits.
