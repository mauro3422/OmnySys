# v0.9.463 - Canonical Status Observability Split and Drift Cleanup

Date: 2026-04-19

## Summary

This release moves the live status observability assembly into the canonical `status-summary` layer and removes the last active watcher drift around the status enrichment path.

## What changed

- Moved the status observability bundle logic into `src/shared/compiler/status-summary/summary.js`.
- Reduced `src/layer-c-memory/mcp/tools/details.js` back to a coordinator that only orchestrates status enrichment.
- Removed the lateral `status-observability-assembly.js` helper once its logic was canonicalized.
- Kept live status and health surfaces wired through the fresh observability runtime.

## Result

- The watcher no longer reports active alerts on the status observability path.
- `details.js` is back under the complexity and file-size budget.
- The observability assembly now lives in the canonical summary layer instead of a private leaf helper.

## Notes

- The system is still watchful at the control-plane level because of broader policy coverage and adoption drift, but this specific status observability path is clean again.
